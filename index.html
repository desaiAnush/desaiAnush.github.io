01

PSU Apartment Price Fairness Tool

deployed

Tell it the rent, beds, distance to campus, and amenities — it tells you if the landlord is charging fair market or trying to pull one over on you. Trained on ~100 real State College listings I scraped from four sites, geocoded, and cleaned.

problem
Penn State students overpay on rent because there's no easy way to price-check a listing.
stack
Python · BeautifulSoup · SQLite · Pandas · scikit-learn · XGBoost · geopy · Streamlit · Plotly
result
Predicts fair rent within $200 on most listings (CV R² 0.46, MAE $198).
